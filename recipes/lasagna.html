Ingredients

  • 200 g fresh lasagna
  • 250 g ground beef
  • 125 ml tomato puree
  • 125 ml red wine
  • 15 g butter
  • onion,carrot,celery
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 sachet saffron
  • 1 pack of bechamel
  • nutmeg
  • salt and pepper

Directions

Chop the onion, slice the celery, dice the carrot and brown everything in the butter and oil. Pour in the meat, tomato puree, wine, parsley and season with salt and pepper. Add 1/2 sachet of saffron and continue cooking for 45 minutes.

Heat the béchamel, dissolve the remaining saffron and nutmeg and leave to cook for 1 minute. Add the nutmeg, season with salt and pepper and remove from the heat.

Heat the oven to 180°. Grease a baking tray with butter and spread a layer of ragù, one of béchamel and one of lasagne on the bottom. Proceed in layers and finish with the béchamel. Sprinkle the lasagna with parmesan and bake for 35/40 minutes. Remove from the oven and serve.
